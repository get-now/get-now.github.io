Everything At
Your Fingertips

The daily requirements of a teacher and a parent can be completely different. That is why we have personalized apps for each of them. But the School Admin Dashboard, Parent App and The Teacher App are all internally connected so that they work seamlessly to meet the user needs.

School Admin
Dashboard

The School Admin Dashboard is a website that allows the administration to control all the activities of the school.

Features

  • Keep complete track of your school bus fleet and plan routes.
  • Coordinate and control everyday activities of teachers and students.
  • Give push notifications to the parents.
  • Create easily usable staff directory.
  • Coordinate all school activities with the complete school calendar.
  • Give notifications about fee payment to the parents.
  • Create posts about upcoming school events.
  • Inform parents in case of emergencies.
  • Send notes and documents to parents.
  • Create a secure space for discussion for teachers and students.
  • Two factor attendance of students.

Parent App

All parents can sign in to the android app to access information about their children.

Features

  • Get complete insights on yours child's performance in school.
  • Track school bus and get arrival and pickup notifications.
  • Know the everyday activities of the school.
  • Keep in touch with all the teachers and school administration.
  • Regular notes are sent to the parents and mobile app ensure that they always receive them.
  • Timely information about upcoming school events.
  • Easily access attendance reports.
  • Analyse the marks of your ward during each exams with detailed improvement graphs.
  • Get timely notifications.
  • Access latest newsletters and gallery images.
  • Obtain important phone numbers and email ids.
  • Keep track of the school calendar.
  • Get notes and question papers of each subject.
  • Up-to-date exam time tables.
  • Apply for leaves through the app.
  • Encourage co-curricular activities of your child through daily monitoring.

Teacher App

The Teacher App is an android application that allows teachers to update and view information from anywhere, anytime.

Features

  • Get complete insights about each student of the class.
  • Manage different students of different classes.
  • Update attendances and marks of each student.
  • Get graphs on each student's performance.
  • Update everyday taken portions and notes.
  • Connect with every student and their parents on a daily basis.
  • Clear doubts of students with individual and group messaging.
  • Keep parents updated about the day to day important events
